Common questions.

Is there a subscription fee?
Vela offers flexible pricing — start free on Explorer, or upgrade to Premier ($99/mo) or Elite ($499/mo) for autonomous booking and full concierge service. No markups on bookings ever.
Does Vela mark up the bookings it makes?
Never. Vela charges only the stated service fee. All flights, hotels, and experiences are booked at market rate. We earn on the planning fee, not by inflating your trip cost.
How does autonomous booking work?
For Premier and Elite clients who grant booking permission, Vela's agents confirm flights, hotels, transfers, and dining using your stored payment method — with a full log of every action taken.
How is my passport and card information protected?
All sensitive credentials are stored in an AES-256 encrypted vault with zero-knowledge architecture. Vela agents access credentials only at the moment of booking and never expose them in logs.
What if I need to change my trip after planning?
Explorer gets 2 revision rounds. Premier and Elite clients get unlimited modifications — including real-time changes while the trip is underway, handled by our agents or dedicated concierge.
Can I use Vela for a $500 weekend trip?
Absolutely. Vela's budget optimization engine excels at stretching limited budgets. Explorer tier is designed exactly for this. The same intelligence that plans a $500K yacht charter helps maximize a $1,000 city break.
