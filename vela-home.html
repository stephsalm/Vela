The Process

From dream to departure in minutes.

Vela's agents work autonomously in the background — searching, comparing, booking, and confirming — while you live your life.

01

Describe your dream

Tell Vela everything — your destination, travel style, who you're going with, what matters most to you. Use casual language, bullet points, or stream of consciousness. The AI understands it all.

02

Set your parameters

Input your budget range (or select "no limit"), travel dates, number of travelers, and your tier. Optionally provide your passport details and payment method for fully autonomous booking.

03

Agents activate

Eight specialized AI agents simultaneously search flights, accommodations, transfers, dining, and experiences — cross-referencing availability, optimizing for your preferences, and surfacing the best options.

04

Review & approve

Within minutes, receive a complete itinerary with all options ranked and explained. Approve autonomously or customize any element. For Elite clients, Vela simply acts.

05

Everything confirmed

Bookings are made, confirmations sent to your encrypted travel vault, documents organized, and your journey timeline synced across all your devices. All you do is pack.
